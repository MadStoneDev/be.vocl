3:04am. She's finally down. I am so tired my eyes have a heartbeat.

I keep these notes because everyone told me "it goes so fast" and I didn't believe them and now I'm terrified they're right. So: tonight she gripped my finger like she was hanging off a cliff. She smells like warm bread. At 2am she did a sneeze so violent she startled herself and then looked betrayed.

I have not brushed my hair in a way I'd describe as "successful" since Tuesday.

I love her so much it frightens me. I also googled "is it normal to miss being bored." (It is. Apparently.)

Future me, when you read this: you made it. Go to sleep.

If you're in the thick of it too — what's the small thing tonight that got you through?
